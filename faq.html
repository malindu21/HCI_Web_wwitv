  • Basics

  • How do I change my password?

    Click in the top right of WWITV.com, And select Settings & Privacy > Settings , Then Click Security and Login. After Click Edit next to Change password. Then Enter your current password and new password. Finally Click Save Changes.

  • How do I sign up?

    Visit wwitv.com/signup. Choose the plan that's right for you. You can downgrade or upgrade at anytime. Create an account by entering your email address and creating a password. Enter a payment method. That's it. Stream on!

  • How to change channels on wwitv?

    For this move, you'll need to log into your WWITV account on your computer or the WWITV mobile app. Head to Settings > Live guide. There you can drag to reorder the channels as they appear in the Live view of your channel guide.

  • Mobile

  • Can I watch tv on my phone?

    If you're using a phone or tablet, just open the WWITV app to start streaming. Note that you do need to be connected to Wi-Fi or using cellular data to use the WWITV app

  • Can I listen to the radio on WWITV?

    Your radio is now broadcasting live on WWITV. Would you like to speak live on air? It's easy, simply open the live broadcasting software of your choice and do your show as usual

  • Can I change the channel on my TV with my phone??

    Yes you can change TV channels using an android phones, All you need to have is a Smart TV that supports android apps on Play store. LG smart TV's in particular have their custom app on play store and even LG android phones have built in TV remote controls apps that work when connected with Wi-Fi.

  • Account

  • How do I delete my account?

    Open the WWITV on your Device. Select the left menu. Select your account icon to open the accounts page. Select your account from the list and click Remove Account.

  • How do I change my billing account settings?

    Tap on your profile photo. Tap Settings. Billing . Tap Payment method or Update to review, change, or add a new form of payment. View your order history. Select any charge to see more details..
